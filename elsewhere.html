HERO

Monira Al Qadiri

A monumental 25-meter mural of a colossal oil tanker dominates the gallery space — painted in dramatic shades of black and blood red, it floats like a ghost from the future.

The Work

In her solo exhibition HERO, Kuwaiti artist Monira Al Qadiri transforms the oil tanker into a modern mythological figure. The ship is both hero and villain — a symbol of prosperity, extraction, and inevitable collapse.

Surrounding the mural are sculptural miniatures of tankers and oil droplets, creating an immersive environment that questions humanity's complicated relationship with fossil fuels.

Exhibition Period

11 July 2025 – 17 August 2026
Berlinerische Galerie, Berlin

Curated as part of a larger investigation into the social, cultural, and environmental legacies of the oil industry.

The exhibition continues Al Qadiri's practice of using sculpture, video, and large-scale painting to examine the hidden infrastructures that shape our world.
