## Affirms

### Sentences hold when the world refuses to

Faith shape: **calibration and witness** — not that the center will hold by itself.

### Evidence and structure over comforting myths
Source: `/blog/harassment`

Rejects: *if I get senior enough, powerful enough, established enough, this will stop.*

> The belief that seniority confers immunity is false at every level of the evidence.
> …it makes them wait for a threshold that does not exist.

Also: *A threat is evidence.* *Tell one person you trust… To create a witness and a timestamp.*
Closing charge: **Name it. Write it down. Do not leave quietly.**

### Power as infrastructure, not permission
Source: `/blog/third-culture-power`

Posture: *Work is serious. Bodies are not decoration. Power is not automatically male. Fairness is not a luxury. Clarity is not coldness.*

> …power that is only recognized in male grammar is not power. It is permission.

Grasping power: *Names on commits. Contracts. Numbers you checked yourself. Text that does not disappear… A public archive that does not depend on the grace of a room.*

> …writing her own. Line by line. Commit by commit. Essay by essay. Until the room is forced to read her as complete.

No handed manual — **write your own**.

### Trust the kiln (process you cannot undo)
Source: `/blog/pate-de-verre`

Dek: *a kiln you have to trust.*
> …I lose all control, which is the hard part.
> …glass makes you wait and means it.
> Made by hand, not by prompt.

### Measurement instead of a leap of faith
Source: `/blog/wire-speed`

> …answer by measurement, instead of a leap of faith.

Same logic as the harassment essay: verify; do not jump.
